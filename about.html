ABOUT US

Exprexpro is a leading logistics enabling company that is aimed at conneting logistics services providers and services users together in ensuring smooth and swift delivery of bulk and single packages. We aim to build the operating system for commerce, through a combination of world-class infrastructure, logistics operations of the highest quality and cutting-edge engineering and technology capabilities. Our services include instant parcel delivery, food delivery, ride-hailing, car and van delivery for large items and more. Currently headquartered in Lagos, We are in the process of expanding to other cities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to enable customers to operate flexible, reliable and resilient supply chains at the lowest costs. We provided supply chain solutions to a diverse base such as e-commerce, marketplaces,enterprises and SMEs across Nigeria. This is achieved through high-quality logistics infrastructure and network engineering, a vast network of domestic and global partners and significant investments in automation, all of which are orchestrated by our self-developed logistics operating system that drive network synergies within and across our services and enhance our value proposition to customers.
